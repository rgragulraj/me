## Abstract

I competed in the MakeX Robotics Competition, a globally recognized robotics challenge with hundreds of teams from over 60+ countries. Representing my team in the Under-19 category in India, we won the national championship (U19) and qualified for the 2019 MakeX World Championship in Guangzhou, China.

MakeX is one of the top STEAM robotics competitions worldwide, emphasizing robot design, strategy, and real-world problem solving.

---

## Content

### Role & Contributions

As a core team member, I contributed to:

- Robot design and prototyping for task challenges
- Embedded system integration for sensors and actuators
- Strategy development and iteration under competitive constraints
- Team coordination during national and international rounds

### Key Outcomes

- **National champion** in India (U19 category)
- **International qualification** for the 2019 MakeX World Championship
- Experience competing against elite teams from 60+ countries in a top-tier international robotics event

This experience was foundational in developing my skills in real-world robotics implementation, rapid prototyping, and competitive engineering.
